Isaac

Isaac is the protagonist of the game, a young boy who escapes to the basement to avoid his mother’s wrath. He has balanced stats and starts with 3 hearts.

Magdalene

Magdalene, often called "Maggy," is known for her high health and starts with a Yum Heart that can heal her, which is also why she starts with 4 hearts. She represents Isaac’s nurturing side.

Cain

Cain is a lucky character with higher speed and attack. He starts with the Lucky Foot, which improves the chances of favorable outcomes in the game.

Judas

Judas is a character with high damage output but low health. He starts with the Book of Belial, which increases his attack power temporarily.

Eve

Eve begins with low health and attack but excels in her "Whore of Babylon" form, triggered when her health is low. She also starts with Dead Bird and the Whore of Babylon passive item.
